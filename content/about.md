## Hey, I'm Akis Maziotis

Site Reliability Engineer with 15+ years in infrastructure, cloud, and automation.

By day I deal with Kubernetes, Terraform, Grafana, and CI/CD pipelines. By night I do the same thing — but at home, with Home Assistant, ESPHome, Modbus, and IR blasters.

### What this blog is about

Documenting real-world home automation projects — not tutorials, but actual systems running in my house:

- ☀️ **Solar & energy** — EcoFlow PV surplus EV charging, zero-export automation
- 🌡️ **HVAC** — LG Therma V heat pump via Modbus, Daikin fancoils via Broadlink IR
- 🔐 **Security** — Reverse-engineered Sigma alarm integration ([open source](https://github.com/phoinixgrr/sigma_connect_ha))
- 🔌 **ESPHome** — VBus protocol decoding, boiler monitoring, sensor builds
- 🌦️ **Weather automation** — Forecast-based awning protection

🌳 Everything is self-hosted on a local homelab. The blog itself runs on Hugo, served from a VM behind Cloudflare — and powered by the same solar panels I write about.
